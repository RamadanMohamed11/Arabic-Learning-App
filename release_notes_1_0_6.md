# Release Notes - Version 1.0.6 (Build 11)

## English
**What's New in This Version?**

*   **Improved Voice Performance:** We've completely removed the intentional delays and fixed overlapping audio bugs. The application now speaks to your child immediately and smoothly when entering new activities, making the learning experience much more responsive!
*   **Smoother Navigation:** Fixed an issue where previous audio tracks would sometimes continue playing after moving to a new challenge.
*   **Under-the-Hood Polish:** We've cleaned up multiple internal workings related to the updated Flutter engine (replacing older rendering functions with newer standards) making the app run faster and significantly reducing the chance of unexpected visual glitches or crashes during prolonged tracing and learning activities.
