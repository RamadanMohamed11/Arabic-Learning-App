Release Notes v1.0.9 (Build 14)

[English]
What's New in this Version:
• Added "Stories Hub" for Level 3 Arabic activities.
• Combined existing stories under the unified "Listen & Read" activity.
• Improved UI for Story Reading with sentence-by-sentence highlights.
• Fixed image cropping issues in the reading quiz sections.
• Updated SVG parser to correctly display numbers 5 and 9 in math tracing activities.
• Refined activity descriptions and prepared the layout for upcoming reading and expressing activities.
